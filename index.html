His Lore

Written Lore

  1. Creation
    • Dr. Robotnik designed him to be the Ultimate Life Form and to be capable of creating energy that is vast enough to power weapons he creates.
    • Shadow was created on the Robotnik Space Station, and while he was being tested on and monitored, the Dr.'s granddaughter Maria was also aboard the space station and is the only human to truly be kind and bond with the Hedgehog. The two become fast friends and inseperable.
    • The Space Station was infiltrated by a Government organization that discovered what Robotnik was up to and began to stop the operation. Violence was used and Maria ended up being a victim of this violence, perishing. The Dr. escaped but Shadow was captured and put into crysleep before he could act. Hence, trauma.
  2. Earth Residency
    • Afterwards, Shadow was on Earth in cryosleep till his escape 40 years after the capture. He fled the government facility keeping him trapped and began to wreak havoc in his quest for answers. Dr. Robotnik was in hiding on earth and found the lifeform from his chaos. Shadow remembered him and became loyal to him in a quest for revenge for what had happened to Maria.
  3. Switch to Antihero position
    • Shadow later realized that this life was not what Maria would have wanted for him, and the death of Robotnik alongside his space station that would have blown up the earth helped him realize he needed to change. He nearly died stopping the weapon alongside the blue blur, and afterwards remained on earth to discover himself and what he will do with the rest of his existence. While not a good guy necessarily, Shadow is most accurately described as an antihero because he doesn't always follow the rules but is not inherently evil.
