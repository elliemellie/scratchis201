About Me

How the Business Began.

I am a small business owner! Eliza & Co. was started in 2023 and I was inspired by meeting Tammy Dinh at the Dragon Boat festival. She is the CEO of her own business called Uncomfy Co. that she started in 2020 during Covid, selling her own handmade and designed art. I was able to talk to her during the summer before I began college and was vastly inspired by her. Hence, I started my own small art-based business and have an e-commerce webstore up and running for it! I have completed sales online and vended in person at markets! The business continues to grow slowly and I aim to collaborate with other small businesses along the way! I am taking business classes in school and majoring in Art History & Curatorial Studies.

My business sells handmade art in many forms and aims to bring out the buyers inner child through art.

I also am branching out into designing manufactured products that will be made by others, but designed by me. This allows a wider catalogue of products for my customers, and options for those who cannot afford the price of handmade art by a real human soul, not some trash made by AI.
